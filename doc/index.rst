===============================
Welcome to Lir's Documentation!
===============================

Lir is a set of tools developed in the area of computational electromagnetics,
by the RF Modelling and Simulation Group in the RINCE Institute, Dublin City
University and licensed under the Apache License.

The name Lir comes from an old Irish myth, the `Children of Lir`_, and means
the sea in Old Irish.
